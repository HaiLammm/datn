# CV Scoring Criteria and Evaluation Guidelines

## Overview
This document provides comprehensive guidelines for evaluating CVs/resumes. The scoring system ranges from 0-100, with specific criteria for each evaluation dimension.

## Scoring Dimensions

### 1. Completeness (0-25 points)
Evaluates how complete and well-structured the CV is.

**Excellent (21-25 points):**
- All essential sections present (contact, summary, experience, education, skills)
- Clear and logical organization
- No missing critical information

**Good (16-20 points):**
- Most essential sections present
- Generally well-organized
- Minor gaps in information

**Average (11-15 points):**
- Basic sections present but incomplete
- Some organizational issues
- Notable missing information

**Poor (0-10 points):**
- Missing multiple essential sections
- Poorly organized
- Significant gaps in information

### 2. Experience Quality (0-25 points)
Evaluates the relevance and quality of work experience.

**Excellent (21-25 points):**
- 5+ years of relevant experience OR exceptional achievements
- Clear progression in responsibilities
- Quantifiable achievements with metrics
- Experience directly aligns with target roles

**Good (16-20 points):**
- 3-5 years of relevant experience
- Solid progression shown
- Some quantifiable achievements
- Experience mostly relevant

**Average (11-15 points):**
- 1-3 years of experience
- Limited progression
- Few quantifiable achievements
- Partially relevant experience

**Entry Level (0-10 points):**
- Less than 1 year experience
- No clear progression
- No quantifiable achievements
- Limited relevance to target roles

### 3. Skills Assessment (0-25 points)
Evaluates technical and soft skills presentation.

**Excellent (21-25 points):**
- Comprehensive skill set listed
- Skills clearly categorized (technical, soft, tools)
- Evidence of skill application in experience section
- In-demand skills for target industry

**Good (16-20 points):**
- Good range of skills listed
- Some categorization
- Most skills supported by experience
- Relevant skills present

**Average (11-15 points):**
- Basic skills listed
- Limited organization
- Weak connection to experience
- Some relevant skills missing

**Poor (0-10 points):**
- Few skills listed
- No organization
- No evidence of skill application
- Missing critical skills

### 4. Professionalism (0-25 points)
Evaluates presentation, formatting, and communication quality.

**Excellent (21-25 points):**
- Professional formatting and layout
- Error-free writing
- Appropriate length (1-2 pages)
- Consistent styling throughout

**Good (16-20 points):**
- Generally professional appearance
- Minor errors only
- Appropriate length
- Mostly consistent styling

**Average (11-15 points):**
- Basic formatting
- Some errors present
- May be too long or short
- Inconsistent styling

**Poor (0-10 points):**
- Unprofessional appearance
- Multiple errors
- Inappropriate length
- Poor styling choices

## Total Score Interpretation

| Score Range | Qualification Level | Recommendation |
|-------------|---------------------|----------------|
| 85-100 | Highly Qualified | Strong candidate for senior/leadership roles |
| 70-84 | Qualified | Good candidate for mid-level positions |
| 55-69 | Potentially Qualified | Suitable for entry-level or with additional training |
| 40-54 | Needs Development | Consider for internships or development programs |
| 0-39 | Not Qualified | Does not meet minimum requirements |

## Industry-Specific Considerations

### Technology/IT
- Programming languages proficiency
- Framework and tool expertise
- Project portfolio evidence
- Certifications (AWS, Google Cloud, etc.)

### Business/Management
- Leadership experience
- Project management skills
- Business metrics and KPIs achieved
- MBA or relevant certifications

### Creative/Design
- Portfolio quality
- Tool proficiency (Adobe Suite, Figma, etc.)
- Client/project variety
- Design awards or recognition

## ATS Compatibility Checklist
- Standard section headings used
- No tables or complex formatting
- Keywords match job descriptions
- Contact information clearly visible
- File format: PDF or DOCX
- No images or graphics for critical information
